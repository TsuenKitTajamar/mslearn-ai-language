Tensions Rise as Ukraine Feels Excluded from Diplomatic Talks

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy has expressed frustration over the exclusion of Ukraine from recent diplomatic talks 
between American and Russian officials in Saudi Arabia. Zelenskyy criticized former U.S. President Donald Trump,
suggesting that Trump is influenced by Russian disinformation. This diplomatic snub has raised concerns among Ukraine and
its European supporters that any deal emerging from these talks could be detrimental to Kyiv's interests. 
 
The situation highlights the ongoing complexities and tensions in the Ukraine conflict,
as efforts to resolve the war continue amidst a backdrop of mistrust and geopolitical maneuvering1.
